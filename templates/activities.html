Sport Activities

Games and sports play a vital role in the physical development of a student, it is said that "A healthy mind dwells in a healthy body", therefore here in Sun Shine ample amount of opportunity is given to the students to keep themselves physically sound in order to pursue the scholastic aim. The school provides a wide range sports materials in Cricket, Football discuss, Jablin-throw and many indoor and outdoor games for the students assisted by a qualified PTI. The curriculum of the school is planned in such a way that the student get chance to participate on Sports day on a large scale through inter house competitions held every year.
