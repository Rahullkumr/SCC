About us

Welcome to Sunshine

Sunshine coaching center run by a social organization named "JHARKHAND JAN KALYAN SAMITI" which was formally inaugurated & laid its foundation stone on December 8, 2006 by Honorable Shri DAMODAR DAS Ji, (Industrial Minister, Jharkhand Government). The dream of the honorable Chairman of the Samiti Shri. S.N. Singh Ji, is harmonious development of all aspects of a child's personality to face the challenging world. Sun Shine is a premier institution located in a lush green surrounding covering 2.5 acres of land. Sun Shine Public School is away from the humdrum of life giving a perfect educational atmosphere, well connected with road and rail. Nearest railway station is only 7km from school campus. The school has permanent infrastructural building with a spacious playground spread in 1.5 acres of land, combined with modern teaching aid and carefully drawn faculty members from every corner of the country.


Our Aims & Objective

The school aims at imparting that meets the needs of the life and aspirations of the nation, i. e.

  • To assimilate the ideals of equality, efficiency and excellence.
  • To enable with the over growing challenges of life.
  • To inculcate the strength of character.
  • To eradicate the evils of Casteism, religious intolerance and superstitions.

Our vision

“To impart value based quality education by synthesizing the rich ancient Indian heritage with modern Science and Technology”
